Landing

Our product is GreenPath, a health app for gamers, by gamers. Nowadays there has been an increase in the number of people wanting to be more healthy but simply lack the motivation to do so. By using video game elements we hope to encourage people and keep them coming back. One of the key features of our product is a streak system to keep users coming back to keep their streak active. We will also have fitness based events and challenges to shake up peoples routines. Users will also have the option to take picture of the wildlife in the area and GreenPath will use AI to scan the photo and log what it sees into an index. Users can also spend money on micro-transactions to speed up progression or unlock cosmetics.

About Our Product

Our product, GreenPath, took heavy inspiration from games such as Pokémon Go for the underlying mechanics that allows our game to be an attractive option as a health app.

Pokédex

We have integrated a system similar to the pokédex from Pokémon Go into our app to encourage players to go out and capture pictures of the nature and wildlife in their local areas.

Daily Challenges

Introducing players to daily challenges gives them short-term goals to work towards. Combined with increasing rewards from consecutive completions of daily challenges, players will build a routine of exercise.

Competition

Our game includes regular competitions that allows our players to directly compete with each other on metrics such as distance walked or "pokédex" entries. This competition will encourage players to do more exercise.

Our Market and Customer Base

Target Market

The target audience of our product is gamers of all ages who wish to be active and go out more often but lack the motivation to do so for whatever reason. However we will also try to include non-gamers who wish to be more active in our target audience but we will try to focus a bit more on the gamer audience.

Our market size will be large, due to the substantial gamer audience on mobile platforms. The game will also attract a casual fitness audience, which will contribute to an overall very large market size. We believe that these users are the ones that will take the majority of our userbase.


Competitor Analysis

Pokemon GO is by far our largest and most relevant competitor, as it fulfils similar needs as GreenPath. They are both games with health elements, but the main difference between the two is our focus specifically on the health aspect of the game. In our app, there are specific “fitness based” events and challenges, which are missing from Pokemon GO.

Pokemon GO also has no way of urging the player to stay consistent with their fitness - this is an area we have innovated on with our “streak” system, which will allow players to build up their streak every day, psychologically conditioning them to value the streak they've invested so much time into. All of these reasons set us apart from Pokemon GO, and make us much more health oriented, while still concealing the health part enough with our game mechanics to attract a gamer audience. That is something that Pokemon GO was not able to do properly in our opinion.

Built in health apps in smartphones have largely made the search for a health app obsolete, as they still track steps, activity over time, and occasionally, like “Samsung Health”, provide small rewards in the form of badges or stickers which you can assign to your profile, and share with your friends in a social system. This system works quite well in most situations, as the social pressure of keeping up with friends can be enough to get someone outside and moving.

Advertisments

Rewarded, optional advertisments

The player will be able to choose to watch an ad, which can either grant them access to an item which can help them, a cosmetic, to speed up their progress or even to avoid a loss of progress, or to get another chance at something. The options with rewarded ads are just limitless, which is what makes them such a great option. And since they are optional, they will not sour the experience of the player, and they get to choose when or how they encounter an ad, and they will know that there is a reward at the end.

In-App Purchases

Cosmetic items, personalisation and useful items

In app purchases are a large revenue stream for GreenPath, as players who have invested significant time into building up their streak may want an item to preserve it if they miss a day. There will also be cosmetic items that will enable the player's to express their own identity, whether that be in the form of clothes for your character, or different visual's within their map, GreenPath has something for everyone

In app currency will be the direct purchase, after which the player will be able to use it in the game to redeem all sorts of rewards

Subscriptions

Seasonal rewards gained through a premium season pass

Image from the hit video game "Fortnite"

Like with many apps following a freemium model, subscriptions are our most valueable revenue stream, as it can generate numbers month after month. Subscriptions will be based around a season pass, in which you will have access to unique premium rewards within the app that may be hard or even impossible to attain otherwise. The player will still have to play and earn these rewards, which means that a user who has bought our subscription will still stay around and play just as much, if not more than a free player, as they now feel that their money needs to be made "worth it"
