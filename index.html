About Us

  • 2004-2006

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Our long journey to making GreenPath began in the mid 2000s when we were all born. From a young age we all showed great enthusiasm and talent towards all things technical.

  • September 2024

    Early Cooperation

    Our group began collaborating together for some assignments and projects shortly after our enrollment in DCU in September 2024. This laid the groundwork for GreenPath to become a thing.

  • January 2025

    Beginning of DIME labs

    In January 2025 we were first assigned to a group with each other for the DIME project. This was the first time that we were all a part of a group together and was the true beginning of GreenQuest.

  • April 2025

    Project Due Date

    In April 2025 the finished product for our DIME project was due. Several months of hardwork from our team had lead up to this and we were all very satisfied with our final submission.

About Our Product

Our product, GreenPath, took heavy inspiration from games such as Pokémon Go for the underlying mechanics that allows our game to be an attractive option as a health app.

Pokédex

We have integrated a system similar to the pokédex from Pokémon Go into our app to encourage players to go out and capture pictures of the nature and wildlife in their local areas.

Daily Challenges

Introducing players to daily challenges gives them short-term goals to work towards. Combined with increasing rewards from consecutive completions of daily challenges, players will build a routine of excercise.
